### PSD申请

**5. PSD Pre-application Evaluation (PSD Application)**

In principle, applicants who have graduated (or are expected to graduate) from an overseas university (undergraduate program) must apply for a **PSD Pre-application Evaluation** via the Kyushu University Pre-admission Support Desk (hereinafter referred to as "PSD") by **23:00 on Sunday, April 26, 2026 (JST)**. Applicants must obtain a result of "Eligible to Apply" prior to submitting their formal application. However, individuals currently enrolled as research students at the Graduate School of Information Science and Electrical Engineering (ISEE) at Kyushu University, or those selected as MEXT Scholarship students (Japanese Government Scholarship), are exempt from this requirement.

Please note that results from PSD Pre-application Evaluations conducted in previous years are invalid; therefore, those who have applied in the past must apply again.

Furthermore, the PSD Pre-application Evaluation only determines whether an applicant is eligible to apply and does not guarantee admission. A separate application must be submitted via the Web Application System. When applying, you must enter your **PSD Account ID number** and the **date you received the "Eligible to Apply" notification** in the designated sections of the Web Application System.

For details regarding the Pre-admission Support Desk application (PSD Pre-application Evaluation), please refer to the following URL:

https://www.isc.kyushu-u.ac.jp/intlweb/admission/pre-admission

To create a personal account for the application, please use the following URL:

https://pss.kyushu-u.ac.jp/
